Registration


Welcome to the registration page for UDMS 2017! Please have a look at the diagram below to find out what to do and how much you need to pay.

Step 1: Scholarship application (optional)

If you are a young researcher, professor or student, you can apply for a scholarship of an 80%, 50%, or 30% reduction of the standard fee or for the student fee for the conference and workshop. If you are a co-author of an accepted paper and one of your co-authors has already paid the standard fee, you can directly have a 30% reduction on the standard fee. Read more here.

Step 2: Payment

Pay the appropriate conference fee listed below (minus any obtained scholarship/reduction).

  • Standard fee: 350 euros or 7300 Mexican pesos
  • Student fee (conference and workshop): 25 euros or 500 Mexican pesos
  • Student fee (conference, workshop and hackathon): 35 euros or 700 Mexican pesos
